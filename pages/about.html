Arkivist.org, the home of blockchain backed archive data.

The mission of Arkivist.org is to create an archive of literature and other media on the blockchain in order to preserve it forever.

What is Arkivist.org?

Arkivist is a play on the word archive, but it also serves as an ark for data.
It is an ark designed to leverage block chain to save information in a decentralized, immutible format.
Why does this matter? In my time we are currently "burning" books via censorship and centralized control.
Many people have been silenced because they present ideas that are not approved by the tech oligarchy class.
This site is a hybrid approach to centralized and decentralized storage.

What are the advantages of using block chain such as what Arkivist does?

  • Immutible = can never be changed.
  • Blockchain is forever.
  • Decentralized. No single point to be shut down.
  • Arkivist uses the Ether-1 blockchain.
